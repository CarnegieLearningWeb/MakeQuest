MakeQuest

At Globaloria we believe that making games is a fun and powerful way to learn. Everyone can write real code, even in your first hour!

In the MakeQuest game, you edit JavaScript code to defeat "404" the evil villain. Along the way, you master computer science concepts like variables and functions.

Remix and expand the full game code in the Sandbox to create your own game. Then publish and share it with your friends!

Designed for:

  • Beginner coders, age 10 and up
  • Chromebook, PC or Mac (all modern browsers)*

Teachers: here is a Lesson Plan for using MakeQuest in your class.

*Not for mobile phones or tablets
